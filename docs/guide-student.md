## Getting Started

### Logging In

1. Open the DAI-NSCT portal in your browser
2. Enter your IUB email address (format: `rollnumber@iub.edu.pk`)
3. Enter your password and click **Sign In**

> **First Login:** You will be required to set a new password before accessing the system. Your new password must be at least 8 characters, contain one uppercase letter, and one number. You cannot skip this step.

---

## Your Dashboard

After logging in you will see your **Dashboard**, which shows:

- **Total Attempts** — how many exams you have completed
- **Average Score** — your average score across all attempts
- **Best Score** — your highest score ever

Below the stats you will find a **Start Exam** button and your full **Attempt History**.

---

## Taking an Exam

### Before You Start

1. Click **Start Exam** on your dashboard, or click **Take Exam** in the sidebar
2. Review the exam information:
   - Number of questions (e.g. 100)
   - Time limit (e.g. 100 minutes)
   - Subject distribution (how many questions come from each subject)
3. Read the exam rules carefully
4. Click **Start Exam Now** when you are ready

> **Important:** Once you start, the timer begins immediately and cannot be paused.

### Exam Rules

- The exam cannot be paused once started
- The exam will auto-submit when the timer expires
- Questions and answer options are randomised uniquely for you
- You can navigate freely between questions at any time
- You can change your answers before submitting
- Results are shown only after you submit

### During the Exam

**Header bar** (always visible at the top):
- Progress bar showing answered vs total questions
- Question counter (e.g. Q 5/100)
- Countdown timer — turns red and pulses when less than 5 minutes remain
- **Submit** button

**Answering a question:**
1. Read the question carefully
2. Click on your chosen answer option — it will highlight in blue
3. You can click a different option to change your answer
4. Use **Previous** and **Next** buttons to navigate
5. Use the dot navigator (numbered dots) to jump to any question directly

**Answered questions** appear as green dots in the navigator.
**Unanswered questions** appear as grey dots.

### Submitting

**Manual submit:**
1. Click the **Submit** button in the top right
2. A confirmation message will show how many questions you have answered
3. Click **OK** to confirm and submit

**Auto-submit:**
- When the timer reaches zero, the exam submits automatically
- Status will show as **Timed Out**

---

## Your Results

After submitting, your results appear immediately:

- **Score percentage** (large, colour-coded)
- **Grade** — Excellent (80%+), Good (65%+), Pass (50%+), Fail (below 50%)
- **Correct** — number of correct answers
- **Wrong** — number of wrong answers
- **Total** — total questions
- **Time taken**

Click **Back to Dashboard** to return to your home page.

---

## Reviewing Your Exam

If your teacher or administrator has enabled exam review, you can see all questions and correct answers after completing an exam.

1. Go to your **Dashboard**
2. Click on any completed attempt to expand it
3. Click **Review Questions & Answers**

The review page shows:
- Each question with all options
- **Green** = correct answer
- **Red** = your wrong answer (if you selected incorrectly)
- **Skipped** questions show the correct answer with a note

> If the Review button is not visible, exam review has not been enabled by your administrator.

---

## Sharing Your Report

You can share any completed exam attempt with anyone (e.g. a parent or supervisor) using a secure link.

1. Go to your **Dashboard**
2. Expand any completed attempt
3. Click **Share Report**
4. A unique link and 16-character password are generated
5. Share the link and password separately with the recipient

**The recipient:**
1. Opens the link in any browser (no login required)
2. Enters the password
3. Views your full exam report

> The same link and password are shown every time you share the same attempt — sharing again does not create a new link.

## Attempt History

Your dashboard shows all your past attempts. Each row shows:
- Date and time of the attempt
- Score badge (green = pass, red = fail)

Click any attempt to expand and see:
- Exact score percentage
- Correct answers out of total
- Status (Completed or Timed Out)
- Review and Share buttons (if available)

## Tips for the Exam

- **Answer all questions** — unanswered questions count as wrong
- **Watch the timer** — it turns red when 5 minutes remain
- **Use the dot navigator** to quickly check which questions you have skipped
- **You can change answers** at any time before submitting
- **Each attempt is unique** — questions and option order are randomised fresh every time

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I pause the exam?**
No. Once started, the exam runs continuously until you submit or the timer expires.

**Q: What happens if my internet disconnects?**
Your answers are saved automatically every time you select an option. If you reconnect quickly, your progress should be intact. The timer continues running in the background.

**Q: Can I take the exam more than once?**
Yes. You can take unlimited attempts. Each attempt generates a fresh set of questions.

**Q: Why can't I see the Review button?**
Exam review must be enabled by your administrator. Contact your teacher or department if you need access.

**Q: I forgot my password. What should I do?**
Contact your teacher or the system administrator to reset your password.
